AI Humidifier Tutorial

Method for automatically humidifying when weather is too dry.

Get Started

The Principle

Use THERMOMETER to measure indoor humidity and send the data to the GATEWAY. The gateway turns on the HUMIDIFIER when the humidity is below 40,and turn off when higher than 70.

Setup Gateway

LAST STEP!

Make a "Smart Sense" to Control the Humidifier Automatically

Click the "Automation" at the bottom of the Mijia, then the "+" button on the right top, find and click on your thermometer at the list, choose "Below set humidity", select a proper data, just like 30%. Find your humidifier at the next page and choose on it, then "Turn on".
